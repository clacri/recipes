Ingredients:
 - 400g chickpeas (boiled already)
 - 12 cherry tomatoes
 - 1 large avocado or 2 small
 - A small can of sweetcorn
 - For dressing, lemon, olive oil and salt

Directions:
Cut the tomatoes in half parts or quarters, and the avocado in small squares.
Mix the well drained chickpeas with the tomatoes and avocado as well as with the sweetcorn
Put the dressing in the salad, and ready to go. This is very nice for a quick meal to bring to work.
